Our Mission

Building a More Informed World

TruthCheck was founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to reliable tools for verifying information. In an era of rampant misinformation, AI can be a powerful ally in the fight for truth.

Our Story

Started by a Team of AI Researchers

TruthCheck began as a research project studying the linguistic patterns that distinguish real journalism from fabricated content. What started as an academic paper became a platform used by hundreds of thousands globally.

We train our models on diverse datasets and continuously improve them based on user feedback and new misinformation trends. Our commitment is to accuracy, transparency, and accessibility.
